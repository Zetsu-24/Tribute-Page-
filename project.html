Biography

Sahabzade Irfan Ali Khan better known by his stage name "Irrfan" was an Indian Actor and his career spanning over 30 years he has done around 85 films including Hindi, English, Marathi and Telugu Cinema. After passing out from National School of Drama(NSD) Irrfan made his debut from the film "Salaam Bombay!" in 1988. Irrfan got critics acclaims and commercial success after his work in Haasil and Maqbool. He was awarded the Padma Shri for his standalone performance in Paan Singh Tomar(2011) His other notable roles include Slumdog Millionaire (2008), New York (2009), Haider (2014), and Gunday (2014), and the television series In Treatment (2010). His Highest grossing film was the comedy-drama Angrezi Medium(2017) which collected a box-office collection of Rs. 322.4 Cr worldwide for which he won the "Filmfare award for the best Actor" in 2018.
He has also done supporting role in the Hollywood films The Amazing Spider-Man (2012), Life of Pi (2012), Jurassic World (2015), and Inferno (2016) Khan was diagnosed with a neuroendocrine tumour.He died at the age of 53 on 29 April 2020 due to a colon infection and He was admitted to Mumbai's Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital on 28 April 2020 and died the following day. He was buried at the Versova Kabristan in Mumbai.
